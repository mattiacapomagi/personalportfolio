UTE Limited edition

Client RUFA x Jungle Juice Brewing
Category Packaging Design
Year 2024/25

Packaging design for UTE, a Keller Pils by Jungle Juice. The concept captures summery, easygoing vibes through a playful mix of vector art and photography. The can features a back-view figure with branding integrated into the swimwear, while the box references classic ice cooler aesthetics.
