UTOPIA cover redesign

Client RUFA
Category Brand/Editorial Design
Year 2024/25

Translating the sonic complexity of Utopia into a visual system. The cover acts as an extension of the album, using spectrograms to visualize frequencies. A clash of handwritten text and sharp sans-serif typography underscores the contrast between organic sound and structural experimentation.
