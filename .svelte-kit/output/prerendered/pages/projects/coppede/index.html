Coppedè: un'enciclopedia urbana

Client RUFA
Category Editorial Design
Year 2025

An alternative guide to Rome’s Coppedè district, decoding its dense symbolic language. Beyond the striking aesthetics, this publication identifies and explains the architectural symbols embedded in the district’s fabric. Paired with a functional minimap, the layout serves as a compact tool for navigating and understanding the visual complexity of Gino Coppedè’s vision.
